Bar Ilan Software Support - Displaying Varying Amounts of Text


 

To vary the amount of text in the Bar Ilan Responsa Project displayed when viewing your search results, choose the appropriate option from the View menu of the search results window.
   

ClickTo view
Number of Lines; then specify the number of lines desired and click OKExcerpts of custom length
LongLong excerpts of text (3-5 lines)
BriefBrief excerpts of text (1 line)
SourcesA list of sources, without any text

 

   

•   

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

These display options also determine how many sources are displayed on screen at any given time: the longer the excerpt of text, the fewer results you can display on screen at one time. Choosing Long enables you to view about five results at one time, while Brief enables you to view about 14 search results, and Sources enables you to display a list of about 28 sources.
•  Within the Bar Ilan Responsa CD these options can also be accessed by right-clicking the search results window and choosing the appropriate option/.
